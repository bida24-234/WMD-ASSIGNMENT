Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Shipping within Botswana typically takes 3-5 business days. International shipping may take 7-14 business days, depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. Please see our Returns & Exchanges page for details.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to select international destinations. Please check our Shipping & Delivery page for more information.
